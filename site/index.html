FAQ

The questions drivers ask first.

Will it fit my truck?
Yes. Brake Checker Pro is adjustable to fit class 7-8 commercial rigs with both free-swinging and floor-mounted brake pedals. Kenworth, Peterbilt, Freightliner, Volvo, Mack, International. If you drive a semi, it fits.
Is it DOT-approved?
Brake Checker Pro is a driver-side inspection tool, not regulated equipment on the truck. It helps you comply with FMCSA Part 393 brake light and air system pre-trip requirements by making the walkaround inspection possible solo.
How long does it last?
Steel construction with rubber feet. Drivers have been running the same unit for five-plus years. There's nothing to wear out. The pedal and seat-bracket end-caps can be replaced if they ever do.
What if it doesn't work for me?
30-day no-questions-asked returns on unused tools. If your cab geometry doesn't accept it or the workflow doesn't fit your route, send it back for a full refund.
Shipping and turnaround?
Ships from Crosby, Texas. Two to five business days continental US. Tracking emailed when it leaves the warehouse.
Can I use it on a trailer too?
Yes. The whole point is that the tool holds your tractor's brake system pressurized while you walk both the tractor and the trailer. Air lines, glad hands, ABS lights, and every brake lamp on the combo.
Is there a warranty?
A 30-day defect guarantee covers any manufacturing flaw — we'll replace it or refund you, no questions. Beyond 30 days, steel doesn't quit. Drivers have been running the same unit for five-plus years without issue. If a rubber foot ever wears, email us and we'll send a replacement.

Yes. If you're outfitting a fleet or buying multiple units, email sales@brakecheckerpro.com with how many you need and we'll send back a quote. We work with owner-operators running 2 trucks all the way up to fleet managers with 50+.
